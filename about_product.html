Questions and Answers

  • Can I put my Body Hide Cover Cape in the washing machine?
    • The suit is a durable synthetic fiber that can be machine washed using the right detergents and techniques. To prevent damage from heat, it's best to use cool or warm water when washing.
  • Will 100% polyester shrink when washed?
    • Your Cover Cape should turn out fine if you use cool or lukewarm machine cycles. However, using hot water may cause slight shrinkage.

Verification: EN343

  • EN 343 is a European Standard for protective clothing that specifies the minimum protection level against wet weather. It considers the entire garment's construction as well as the fabric used.
