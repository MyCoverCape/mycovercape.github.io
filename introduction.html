Introduction

Critical need for a product

Learn More

Being involved with children and safeguarding as a teacher, respite carer and father, it struck me there was no protective garment for children. As a gym user, we are all required to use the facilities provided for our health and hygiene such as changing areas, changing rooms, showers etc. When taking my daughter to swimming I realised it was hard for her to have her post swimming shower without the risk of unwanted eyes on her. While there is a focus on what happens in the Pool, I realised from my research there is not enough thought or attention as to what happens outside the pool which can have an impact on the safe guarding of our children.

This site has been formed as a source of information using well-known UK companies which share the importance of safeguarding as we do in which a product had been created to mitigate the safeguarding concerns found through our research.

Our Mission


To safeguard all who need care


Our vision


To set safe guarding boundaries and restore modesty back in our society
