Research plays a pivotal role in safeguarding children in sports environments by informing best practices, policies, and interventions that can help create safe and secure spaces for young athletes. By conducting thorough research, engaging with stakeholders, and implementing evidence-based strategies, we can work towards ensuring the well-being and protection of children in sports clubs and centres.


  • We researched a number of companies linked to better safeguarding
  • We went out on the field to look at real sports centres / clubs
  • We asked questions linked child safety and protection in a sports environment.
  • We demonstrated our product and received feedback from parents and children
  • We met with compliance professionals in relation to the product to ensure the correct standards are met
  • Factoring all the above, we were determined to create a safeguarding product which will aid specific safeguarding fundamentals

If you have any further questions or would like more information about our research initiative, please feel free to contact us where we will be happy to present our findings and show you the product!
